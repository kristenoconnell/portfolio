About Me

At Thinkful, I've just begun to dip my toes into JS, HTML, and CSS, and I have many more exciting things coming up. I'm always in the market for new knowledge, a mentor, friend, or another new-to-coding buddy! Please feel free to reach out to me through one of the links below.
