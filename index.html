Hi! I'm Kristen.

Service-industry extraordinaire turned aspiring web developer, I hail from the not-so-small town of Plano, Texas. I'm most interested in web applications that bring people and ideas together. Please feel free to explore my projects, learn more about me, or get in touch through my page!

About Me

I went to college at the University of Denver and majored in Psychology. I love to read, study, and learn, and I find ,myself drawn to a wide variety of subjects. I've been a problem solver for as long as I can remember, and as any developer can attest - the feeling of solving a function you've been stuck on for way too much time is unmatched.

I love spending time outdoors, hiking mountains and watching sunsets in particular. I am a certified SCUBA diver and care deeply about environmental sustainability. I hope to build web applications that push the envelope, bring people together, and find ways to make the world a better place.
